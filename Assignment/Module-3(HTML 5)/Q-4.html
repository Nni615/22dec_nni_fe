(4) Canvas and SVG tags.

->Canvas and SVG both are graphic's concept.
->Canvas is an pixel based and resolution depended.
->In canvas if the screen size is changed , then pixel of the image will also change.
->More you zoom , more the image will blur or quality will change.
->SVG stands for Scalable Vector Graphics.
->In this the quality of the image will not change in any of the screen size.
->For the graphical representation of the image then SVG and Canvas is use in javascript.
